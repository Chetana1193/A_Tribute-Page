P V sindhu (Pusarla Venkata Sindhu)

Rewrote the History Of Indian Olympics

2009-Onwards

Born 5 July 1995 (age 26) Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh,India

Indian Professional Badminton Player

First Indian to become the Badminton World Champion and second individual athlete at the Olympic Games.

Years Achievements
2009-2011 Sindhu entered the international circuit at age of 14
2012 She won Asian Junior Championships
2013 Sindhu was awarded the Arjun Award by the government of India.
2014 She claimed her first medal at the Asian Championships.
2015 She suffered a stress fracture that kept her away from playing
2016 Sindhu won the Malaysia Masters Grand Prix Gold women's singles.
2017 The first Indian to win Korea Open.
2018 she got the historic first silver medal for India in Women's singles badminton.
2019-2020 Sindhu got a wild card entry into the 2019 BWF World Tour Finals.
2021 Sindhu got a wild card entry in BWF World Tour Finals.
